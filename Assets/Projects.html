Our Projects

We have a wide range of projects available for the public to Participate in.

1. Our first project involves tree planting especially in areas where logging happens or in desert areas to make the earth cooler and enable the carbon cycle to continue in such areas.

2. One of our other projects involves cleaning microplastics from waterbodies such as the ocean this helps to make the water cleaner and safer as well as ensuring all the aquatic animals can have a safe habitat.

3. Similarly one of our other projects involves waste collection in public places to make the environment have fresh air and to prevent the nearby wildlife from being affected.

4. Additionally one of our other projects involves recycling plastics to prevent destruction of the ozone layer by factories that burn plastics on a daily basis.

5. Likewise one of our other projects involves practicing irrigation in barren areas this helps put more land under use to be able to improve food security .

6.Finally another of our other projects involves conducting extensive research into diverse ways of reducing climate change to better our environment.
